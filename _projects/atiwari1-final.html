Thermal

left-click on the grid to attract the bird to that point. left-click and drag to move the grid in the vertical axis (or scroll). right-click and drag to rotate the camera, right click and scroll to zoom.
click on the central sphere to brake. press D to swtich between orthographic and perspective views. shift can also be used instead of right-click.
made by Aman Tiwari, based on Adam Smith and Issac Karth's work on procedural generation.
Created under the aegis of Eddy Man Kim's Spatial Narratives in Web Graphics (2017), with thanks to Ben Scott and Rehan Butt.
